Collaborating Composers

The Jenny Lind Singers actively champions the music of local women composers. We commission new works and perform them alongside repertoire from across the choral tradition.

Kate Hill

Eastnor, Worcestershire

Kate Hill is a composer whose work spans choral, chamber, and orchestral music. Based in Eastnor in the shadow of the Malvern Hills, her writing is distinguished by its lyrical clarity and deep sensitivity to text.

The Jenny Lind Singers have performed several of her pieces, and the creative relationship continues to develop with each season.

Liz Dilnot Johnson

West Malvern

Liz Dilnot Johnson is one of Britain's most acclaimed choral composers. Her music is notable for its emotional directness and its ability to illuminate the full expressive range of the human voice.

"Most impressive of all was Johnson's music, a composition of notable insight, beauty, and variety." — Church Times
