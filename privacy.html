Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Jenny Lind Singers is a choral ensemble based in Malvern, Worcestershire. This page explains what personal information we collect through this website, why we collect it, and what we do with it. We aim to be straightforward about this.

What we collect and why

Enquiry form

When you use the contact form on this site, we receive your name, email address, and the message you send us. We use this only to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your separate consent.

Mailing list

If you sign up to our mailing list, we collect your email address. We use this to send occasional concert announcements and news. You can ask to be removed at any time by emailing us.

How your data is stored

Form submissions are processed by Formspree, a third-party service. Data is stored on their servers in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not sell or share your personal information with any other third parties.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking software. We do not run analytics or advertising on this site.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to ask what information we hold about you, to request that we correct it, and to ask us to delete it. To do any of these things, please get in touch via the contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please use the contact form.
